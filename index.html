About

This site attemps to track consumer drone incidents reported in the United States beginning in 2014. I've sourced data from the FAA1, The Democrat & Chronicle2, and my own reporting.

This is my first ever coding project. Please feel free to contribute on Github. I've released this project under the MIT License.

You can get in touch with me on Twitter @brent1x.
