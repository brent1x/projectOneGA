About

This site attemps to track consumer drone incidents reported in the United States beginning in 2014. I've sourced data from the FAA1, The Democrat & Chronicle2, and my own reporting, but I make no claims to its completeness. If you are aware of an incident, please send it my own via the Report page. I will groom the data over time.

This is my first ever coding project. Please feel free to contribute on Github. I've released this project under the MIT License.

You can get in touch with me on Twitter @brent1x.
